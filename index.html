About

A doctor's take, made for a scroll — not a waiting room.

Dr. Shahzad Mureed makes short-form medical content aimed at closing the gap between what doctors know and what most people are told about their own mental health.

Each video is built to be watched in under a minute, understood in one pass, and shared without a second thought — using the same everyday mix of Urdu and English the audience already thinks in.
